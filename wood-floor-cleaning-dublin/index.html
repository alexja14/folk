We Clean All Types Of Wooden Floors

Hardwood, engineered, parquet, laminate — each wooden floor has different characteristics, and we understand them all. Regular mopping pushes dirt deeper into the grain rather than lifting it. Over time, the finish dulls, scratches accumulate and the floor loses the warmth it once had.

Our wood floor cleaning process uses specialist machines and professional-grade products that are safe for timber. We start with a dry extraction pass, then apply an appropriate cleaning solution, agitate the surface and extract the residue thoroughly — leaving no streaks, no moisture damage.

Where needed, we finish with re-oiling or re-sealing to restore the protective layer, leaving your floor looking and feeling like new.
