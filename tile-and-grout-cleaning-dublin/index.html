We Clean All Types Of Tile & Grout Floors

Grout is one of the toughest cleaning challenges in any home. Its porous surface soaks up everything — grease, dirt, mould, bacteria — and standard mopping only makes it worse by pushing dirty water further into the lines.

We use high-pressure steam combined with specialist grout cleaning solutions to break down the buildup at the source. Our rotary tools scrub the grout lines clean while we simultaneously extract the residue — the result is grout that looks like it was just laid.

To keep it that way, we recommend and apply a professional grout sealer that prevents future absorption, making regular maintenance much easier.
