Advanced Cleaning Methods For Every Floor Type

At Floor Cleaning Squad, we don't believe in cookie-cutter deep floor cleaning. That's why we customise our methods to match the space you’re in, so you get the best result.

Wooden Floors

We restore lacquered and oiled wood floors with floor-safe cleaning methods that lift grime and refresh the finish without harsh treatment. It’s one of the quickest ways to make a tired wood floor look cleaner, richer, and more uniform again.

Marble Floors

Marble needs the right products and the right technique, or it shows every mistake. We offer deep floor cleaning and diamond polishing options to help you achieve a matte, honed, or glossy finish, depending on the stone and the look you want.

Stone Floors

From limestone and travertine to slate and sandstone, we clean stone deep into the pores and restore the finish where needed. We can also recommend sealing options for extra protection, based on your stone type and how the space is used.

Tile & Grout

Tiles can look fine while grout holds years of trapped dirt. We deep scrub and extract grime from tiles and grout lines until the dirt actually comes out, leaving your floor noticeably cleaner and brighter.

Vinyl, Lino & Marmoleum

These types of floors need the right system to look their best long-term. We remove tired buildup and apply a durable protective finish where suitable, so the surface looks cleaner and becomes easier to maintain.

Commercial Floors

High-traffic floors need a cleaning approach that holds up. We clean, polish and protect commercial floors using systems designed for daily wear with scheduling that works around your opening hours.

Frequently Asked Questions

What results can I realistically expect from professional floor cleaning in Dublin?

Proper deep floor cleaning will make a floor look noticeably cleaner, brighter and more uniform, especially once built-up dirt is lifted, and the surface is dried properly.

I’m not sure if my wood floor is oiled or lacquered. Does it matter?

Yes, it matters a lot, and many homeowners don’t realise their floor is oiled, and each finish requires a different process. But don't worry, just send us a photo, and our Dublin floor cleaning team will identify it for you.

Do you offer sealing after cleaning?

Yes, where it makes sense. Some floors benefit hugely from sealing, especially porous surfaces or floors that need added stain resistance. Whatever your case may be, we'll let you know the best option for your floor.

Do your products create fumes or produce strong smells?

Modern products are far less harsh than they used to be. That said, there may be a smell during and shortly after the job, but with windows open and proper ventilation, it clears quickly. If there are babies or vulnerable occupants, we’ll talk through the safest option.

Do I need to leave the house while you clean?

You’re welcome to stay, but there’ll be a lot going on with machines and products, and it’s easier and safer if you go shopping or even run some errands while we work. We’ll call you in once it’s done so you can see the results.

Do you move furniture?

If we did the moving too, our backs wouldn't survive it. That said, we kindly ask that you move out your furniture before we arrive so everything runs smoothly.

How soon can I book?

We can schedule your floor cleaning in Dublin as early as the next day, depending on your schedule and availability.

What if I’m not happy with the result?

Complaints usually happen when expectations weren’t clear from the start. That's why we're always transparent about what's possible so we never over-promise. If something isn’t right, we’ll talk it through and make it right where possible.

Do you provide floor polishing services?

Yes, we do provide floor polishing services, but only if your floor can handle it. Connect with us to discuss your floor type and the best options for your needs.
