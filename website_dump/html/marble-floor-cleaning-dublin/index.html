Because Marble Floors Show Everything

Marble is one of the most stunning floor choices one can ever make. However, it's the type of flooring that lets everyday life leave its signature on its surface, from dull traffic lanes, etched spots and uneven shine to the grime sitting in pores and grout lines.

The worst part? It reacts badly to the wrong products and the wrong techniques, as acidic cleaners can damage it, and aggressive scrubbing can leave it looking worse.

When cleaning alone is not enough to remove built-up residue, etching, and a dull finish, it’s time to call our Marble floor cleaning Dublin specialists. We tailor the level of restoration to your marble type, your budget, and the finish you want, then carry out the work using stone-safe methods that bring the surface back to life.

Signs Your Marble Floor Needs Professional Cleaning

If you notice any of the following issues in your marble flow, our marble cleaning services in Dublin are here for you.

  • Your floor looks dull or uneven, especially in walkways
  • There are etch marks or water spots that won’t wash out of your marble
  • The surface of your floor feels rough, chalky, or patchy in certain areas
  • Grout lines look darker than they should
  • Your marble never looks clean for long, even after mopping

We Match Our Method To The Marble

A proper marble floor cleaning isn’t about scrubbing harder and hoping for the best. We start by
assessing your marble type and its condition, then match the method to what the stone actually
needs, so you’re never left with damaged, dulled or patchy results.

Pre-Clean Assessment

We inspect the marble type, current finish, and problem areas, from dull patches, staining and traffic lanes to etching.

Budget & Finish Planning

We agree on the level of service, whether that’s deep cleaning only or full restoration and marble polishing.

Deep Clean With Alkaline Cleaner

We machine-clean using an alkaline cleaner to lift grime safely, including grime in grout lines and porous areas.

Grout & Detail Pass

We focus on grout lines, edges and problem zones so the floor looks evenly clean, not just clean in the middle.

Diamond Restoration & Marble Polishing

We use a diamond grit process to refine the surface and restore the finish to matte, honed, or gloss, depending on the marble type and your preference.

Sealing & Aftercare

At your request, we'll apply an invisible or enhancing solvent sealant for extra protection. Once done, we'll give you guidance on how to protect your floor.

We Keep Our Process Straightforward

Our marble cleaning in Dublin is never cookie-cutter, because not every floor needs the full works, and not every client wants the same finish. Send photos of your marble floors and rough measurements, and our team will tell you what outcome is realistic for your floor and give you a quick estimate. If the area is larger or more complex, we’ll arrange a visit and walk you through the best marble restoration service for you.

We’ll also talk you through the trade-offs, if any, from what your budget can achieve and what finish suits your marble type, to whether sealing is worth it for your situation, and what maintenance looks like after the work is done. Either way, we’ll recommend what makes sense for your budget and the result you want.
