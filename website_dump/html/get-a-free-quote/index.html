Our Process Is Straightforward

Anyone can promise great results, but we prefer to show you exactly why our work holds up and why so many Dublin homeowners and businesses book us when they want it done properly, not just quickly.

Get In Touch

Give us a call or drop a message to talk to one of our floor cleaning service specialists. WhatsApp works best if you want a quick response.

Share Photos And Measurements

Send a few clear photos and the rough size of the area. If the job is larger or more complex, we can arrange a visit. 

Receive An Estimate

We’ll send an estimate based on what we can see and schedule the best time to come around for a clean when it's convenient for you.

Confirm Everything Before We Start

On the day of the booked day, we'll confirm the scope, get our cleaning equipment and products out to start the job.

We Complete The Job And Hand Over

We'll clean, finish and inspect our work. Once we're happy, we'll advise you on aftercare so your floor stays looking good for longer.
