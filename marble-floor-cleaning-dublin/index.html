We Clean All Types Of Marble Floors

Marble is one of the most beautiful — and most sensitive — flooring materials. It reacts badly to acidic cleaners, harsh chemicals and abrasive tools. Using the wrong product even once can permanently etch or dull the surface.

Our marble cleaning team use pH-neutral, marble-safe cleaning agents combined with diamond-pad polishing equipment to gently lift surface dirt, restore clarity and bring back the original depth of the stone.

We also offer marble sealing to protect against future staining and etching, giving you peace of mind that your investment is properly protected.
